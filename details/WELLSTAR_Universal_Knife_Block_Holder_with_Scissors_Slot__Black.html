The WELLSTAR Universal Knife Block Holder

Elevate your kitchen organization with a stylish, durable and practical knife block holder that keeps your tools secure, clean and within reach.

Why Choose This Knife Block?

This universal knife block comes with a thoughtful design that accommodates up to 12 knives and even includes a dedicated scissors slot. Whether you're using small paring knives or large Chinese cleavers, there's a perfect space for every tool in your collection.

The double-layer system ensures that your knives are safely nestled and separated, reducing the risk of damage and keeping your countertop looking clutter-free. The detachable top makes cleaning simple and efficient, allowing you to remove and wash the frame easily for hygienic storage.
